About Android

Android is a mobile operating system developed by Google , based on a modified version of the Linux Kernel and other sourcesoftware and designed primarily for touchscreen mobile devices such as smartphonesand tablets. In addtion, Google has further developed Android TV for televisions,Andrid Auto for cars, and Android Wear for wrist watches, each with a specialized user interface. Vaariantsof Android are also used on game consoles, digital cameras,PCs and other electronics.
